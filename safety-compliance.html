Safety, Quality & Environmental Policy

🔒 Our Commitment to Responsible Global Distribution

At Laxmi Dyes Industries, we recognize that the chemicals we supply are the lifeblood of global industry—but they also demand the highest standards of stewardship. Our policy is built on the principle of "Zero Harm, Absolute Purity."

📈 1. Quality Assurance & Product Integrity

  • Batch Consistency: We ensure every shipment of Caustic Soda, Soda Ash, and industrial acids meets the exact technical specifications and purity levels promised in our Certificate of Analysis (COA).
  • Vetted Supply Chain: We only source from manufacturing units that adhere to ISO 9001 standards, ensuring traceability from the production floor to the final delivery.

🛡 2. Dangerous Goods (DG) Safety & Compliance

  • Regulatory Mastery: We operate in strict accordance with the IMDG Code for sea freight and GHS (Globally Harmonized System) for labeling.
  • UN-Certified Packaging: All hazardous materials are packed in UN-Approved drums, IBCs, or ISO tanks, specifically tested to withstand the rigors of international transit and prevent leaks or environmental exposure.
  • Clear Documentation: Every shipment is accompanied by a 16-section, GHS-compliant Safety Data Sheet (SDS) and a Dangerous Goods Declaration (DGD) where required.

🌱 3. Environmental Stewardship

  • Spill Prevention: Our logistics partners are selected based on their ability to handle chemicals without environmental compromise.
  • Resource Efficiency: By optimizing our shipping routes from Gujarat’s ports, we minimize the carbon footprint of our global delivery network.

⚙ 4. Continuous Improvement

  • We commit to regular internal audits of our handling SOPs and stay updated on evolving UAE and international chemical regulations to protect our clients from compliance risks.
